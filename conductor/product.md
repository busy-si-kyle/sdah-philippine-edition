# Product Guide - SDA Hymnal Philippine Edition

## Vision
To provide a digital home for the SDA Hymnal Philippine Edition, making hymns easily accessible for worship, study, and musical preparation. The platform serves as a collaborative bridge between traditional worship and modern digital accessibility, ensuring every hymn is preserved with its lyrics and sheet music.

## Target Users
- **Church Members:** Accessing lyrics and hymns during services or personal devotions.
- **Musicians & Choir Members:** Searching for and downloading sheet music for practice and performance.
- **Contributors:** Dedicated users helping to complete the collection by digitizing and uploading missing sheet music.

## Key Features
- **Advanced Search & Navigation:** 
    - Search by hymn number (contains match), title, or full-text lyrics.
    - Sequential navigation (Previous/Next) between hymns with keyboard shortcuts and mobile swipe gestures.
    - Browse hymns by category.
- **Comprehensive Sheet Music Support:**
    - Dedicated full-page in-browser sheet music viewer for local assets.
    - Status tracking for hymns with and without sheet music.
- **Community Contribution:**
    - A dedicated MuseScore transcription workflow and an integrated issue reporting feature.

## Visual Identity
- **Modern & Minimalist:** A clean, high-contrast interface using sans-serif typography. Priority is given to readability and whitespace to ensure a distraction-free experience for worship and music reading.
